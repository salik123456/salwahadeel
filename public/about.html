Salwa Hadeel Fashion Store

Salwa Hadeel Fashion Store is a Pakistani clothing store which is based in Manama,Bahrain. We offer great collections from various Pakistani cities.The clothing includes stiched, ready-to-wear clothes. We provide clothes with a diverse range of designs and colors. The clothes are available in multiple sizes and pieces. The fabric includes chiffon,organza and lawn which provides customers to choose their prefered style and fabric.

We plan to grow online so that we can provide quality clothes available for locals through various platforms. Our philosophy is to provide high quality clothes to our customers in affordable prices with absolutely no compromise on product's quality which adds confidence in woman's personality.
